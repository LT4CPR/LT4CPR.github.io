NGO Collaborators

ClearGlobal (formerly Translators without Borders) is a non-profit focused on language access and communication for underserved communities in crisis settings. ClearGlobal works closely with organizations such as the Red Cross and governmental agencies around the world, and maintains a dedicated branch for developing language technologies to support humanitarian personnel.

GMU has established a contractual agreement with ClearGlobal for the creation of speech translation corpora in under-resourced languages, grounding the infrastructure development in real operational needs.
