Workshops

The LT4CPR project organizes and co-organizes workshops to build community around language technologies for crisis response, bringing together NLP researchers, crisis informatics experts, humanitarian practitioners, and representatives from affected communities.

The first NLP workshop dedicated to language technologies for crisis preparedness and response. Features invited talks, a panel discussion, and a shared task on automatic situation report generation.
