LT4CPR Workshop Accepted at ISCRAM 2026

We are excited to announce that our workshop Voices Across Crises: Stakeholder Perspectives and Opportunities for AI Speech Technologies has been accepted at the 23rd International Conference on Information Systems for Crisis Response and Management (ISCRAM 2026).

The workshop will take place on Sunday, May 31, 2026 at Leiden University, Campus The Hague, Netherlands. It will bring together crisis management researchers, practitioners, policymakers, and technology researchers to explore how AI speech technologies can support communication during emergencies, with a focus on vulnerable and linguistically diverse communities.

The workshop is participatory and does not require paper submission. All ISCRAM 2026 attendees are welcome.
